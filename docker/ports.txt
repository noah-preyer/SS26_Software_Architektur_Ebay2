Service Ports Overview
======================

Ordered by logical dependency chain:

  Service             | Port  | Internal | Container                | DB Port
 ---------------------|-------|----------|--------------------------|--------
  auth-service        | 8081  | 8080     | auth-service-db          | 5432
  user-service        | 8082  | 8080     | user-service-db          | 5432
  order-service       | 8087  | 8080     | order-service-db         | 5432
  payment-service     | 8086  | 8080     | payment-service-db       | 5436
  notification-service| 8084  | 8080     | notification-service-db  | 5434
  image-service       | 8088  | 8080     | image-service-db         | 5438

All services map host:{Port} → container:8080.
DB ports are the host-side mappings (container:5432).
