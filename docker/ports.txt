Service Ports Overview
======================

Ordered by logical dependency chain:

  Service             | Port  | Internal | Container                | DB Port
 ---------------------|-------|----------|--------------------------|--------
  auth_service        | 8080  | 8080     | auth-service-db          | 5432
  user_service        | 8082  | 8080     | user-service-db          | 5432
  payment_service     | 8084  | 8080     | payment-service-db       | 5436
  email_service       | 8086  | 8080     | notification-service-db  | 5434
  image_service       | 8088  | 8080     | image-service-db         | 5438

All services map host:{Port} → container:8080.
DB ports are the host-side mappings (container:5432).
