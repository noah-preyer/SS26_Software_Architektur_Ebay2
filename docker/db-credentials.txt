Database Credentials & Seed Users
=================================

DATABASE CONNECTIONS
--------------------

Service              | Database              | User                | Password      | Container                | Port
---------------------|-----------------------|---------------------|---------------|--------------------------|------
auth-service         | auth_service          | auth_user           | app_password  | auth-service-db          | 5432
user-service         | user_service          | user_user           | app_password  | user-service-db          | 5432
email-service        | notification_service  | notification_user   | app_password  | notification-service-db  | 5434
payment-service      | payment_service       | payment_user        | app_password  | payment-service-db       | 5436

Pattern: {service}_user / app_password @ {service}-db:{port}/{service}_service

SEED APPLICATION USERS (login credentials)
------------------------------------------

Email                       | Username  | UUID                                   | Password
----------------------------|-----------|----------------------------------------|--------------
admin@example.com           | admin     | 11111111-1111-1111-1111-111111111111  | (use bcrypt hash from auth init)
lukas.weber@example.com     | lukasw    | 22222222-2222-2222-2222-222222222222  | (use bcrypt hash from auth init)

These users are seeded across:
  - auth-service   → users table (credentials, roles)
  - user-service   → user_profiles table (profile info, addresses)

Both share the same UUIDs to maintain cross-service reference.
